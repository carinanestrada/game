How to play
Game rules:
  1. Press to move chickens! button will make both chickens move forward a random distance.
  2. The chicken that reaches the finish line first wins the race!
  3. Your wins are tracked in the stats.
  4. Click new race to reset for another round.
Difficulty modes:
  • Easy: Chickens move 10-30 pixels per click
  • Hard: Chickens move 5-50 pixels per click
